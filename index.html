During Q4, we made a single investment in the sustainable personal care brand, Fussy. Fussy is on a mission to banish single-use plastic from the bathroom and has shown strong revenue growth and high LTV:CAC in their short period of operation. In a recent investor update, Passion Capital who invested in Wild - a similar brand, sighted them as having the highest revenue in their portfolio with a 90% growth rate. We have also been impressed with Fussy's investor communication which suggests to us a brand that is disciplined and proactive.
